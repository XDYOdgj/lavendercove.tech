The Goothdurs Mini Travel Compact Umbrella

Stay prepared for any weather condition with the Goothdurs Mini Travel Compact Umbrella. This sleek, portable rain and sun shield is designed to fit seamlessly into your daily life. Its ultra-compact 6.7-inch folded size makes it easy to carry, whether you're on a road trip, commuting, or running errands. Weighing just 8 oz, it's light enough to slip into your handbag, backpack, or even your pocket without compromising comfort.

When fully extended, this umbrella offers a spacious 37-inch canopy that keeps you protected from both sunlight and unexpected rain. Its upgraded windproof features, including 8 strong fiberglass ribs and a fully metal frame, ensure stability even in tough weather, so you can rely on it during sudden storms or gusty conditions. From bustling city streets to beach days and outdoor adventures, the Goothdurs Mini Travel Compact Umbrella is a trusted companion for everyone.

With 95% UV protection and a UPF 50+ rating, it's an ideal choice for sun safety. The waterproof 210T Teflon material prevents water from seeping in, keeping you dry through heavy downpours and ensuring quick drying after use. Whether you're looking to protect yourself or seeking a stylish essential, this umbrella delivers convenience, durability, and top-quality performance.
