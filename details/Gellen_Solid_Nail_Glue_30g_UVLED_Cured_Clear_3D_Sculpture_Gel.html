Gellen Solid Nail Glue – Perfect for Durable Manicures

Gellen Solid Nail Glue is a powerful, upgraded formula designed for press on and acrylic nails. It offers superior adhesion, keeping your designs strong and secure for longer. With a clear 3D sculpture gel consistency, it's non-sticky and easy to handle, ensuring a smooth and comfortable experience with no messy residue.

Whether you're a pro or a beginner, Gellen's nail art gel is perfect for creating stunning false nail tips or adding sparkle with rhinestones. Its smooth texture and bubble-free application allow for precise positioning before UV or LED curing, making the nail art process more flexible and enjoyable. The long-lasting bond can stay intact for up to 15-30 days with proper use, eliminating the need for frequent touch-ups and keeping your manicure looking fresh and flawless.

  • Non-Sticky & Comfortable for Hands, No Air Bubbles
  • Long-Lasting Bond Up to 15-30 Days with Proper Use
  • Multi-functional for Nail Tips, Rhinestones, and Decorations

Why Choose Gellen?

Gellen is the ideal choice for anyone passionate about nail art. It’s designed to give you professional-grade results at home without hassle. The durable bond and easy handling make it perfect for both everyday wear and special occasions. With a focus on customer satisfaction, Gellen ensures that your manicure remains chip-free and beautiful for days.
