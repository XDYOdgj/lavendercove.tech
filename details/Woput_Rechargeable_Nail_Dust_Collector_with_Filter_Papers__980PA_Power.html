Woput Rechargeable Nail Dust Collector

Elevate your nail care routine with the Woput Rechargeable Nail Dust Collector. Designed for both professionals and hobbyists, this powerful tool ensures you stay dust-free and focused throughout every manicure or pedicure session.

Effortless Dust Removal

Equipped with a high-powered 980PA suction and an 8200RPM turbo fan, this nail vacuum efficiently captures fine dust and debris from your work. Whether you're applying acrylics, doing fill work, or polishing nails, it delivers superior performance for a cleaner workspace.

Long-Lasting Power and Convenience

Thanks to its 5000mAh rechargeable battery, the Woput dust collector offers up to 5 hours of continuous use. Just a quick 2.5-hour charge keeps you ready for all your nail projects, without the annoyance of cords or constant recharging.

The inclusion of 30 disposable filter papers ensures hassle-free maintenance and promotes hygiene. You can easily replace filters when needed, maintaining optimal airflow and suction for each task. Its compact and lightweight design makes it perfect for use in salons, at home, or on the go. The Woput nail vacuum is built to be reliable, user-friendly, and an essential part of your nail art toolkit, helping you achieve smooth and efficient results every time.
