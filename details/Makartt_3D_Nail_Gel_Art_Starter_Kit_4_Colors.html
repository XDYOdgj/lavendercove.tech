Makartt 3D Nail Gel Art Starter Kit

Take your nail art skills to the next level with this complete DIY set. Designed for both beginners and experienced nail artists, this collection offers everything you need to create stunning, long-lasting manicures with ease.

Premium Quality Gels

Featuring 4 solid gel builder colors—clear, glitter, shimmer white, and pink—this kit provides a versatile palette for your creative projects. These gels are ideal for nail extensions, press-on nails, and intricate 3D designs.

Innovative 3D Sculpting Tools

The 2 silicone molds are specifically designed to create detailed love hearts and floral patterns, allowing you to add dimension and elegance to every manicure. They're perfect for achieving that professional look without extra hassle.

Precision Sculpting Pen

Equipped with a dual-ended sculpting pen, this kit ensures precise application and efficient shaping. Whether you're working on fine details or larger designs, the pen offers smooth, controlled results that set your nail art apart.

Convenient & Versatile

Thanks to its nonstick surface and easy manipulation, these gels are simple to work with and perfect for layering. Plus, they can be filed off and reused, making them a cost-effective choice for fill-in sessions and creative experimentation.

Quick Curing with UV/LED Lamp

With just a quick cure using a UV/LED lamp, you can achieve strong, flexible, and long-lasting results. This method is clean, efficient, and ensures your nail art stays fresh and beautiful for days.

A Complete Solution for Any Nail Art Journey

Whether you're new to nail art or already have some experience, this starter kit is the perfect place to begin. It combines functionality, creativity, and convenience to help you craft unique, eye-catching styles that reflect your personality.
